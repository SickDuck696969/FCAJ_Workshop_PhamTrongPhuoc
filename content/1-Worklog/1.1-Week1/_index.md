### Week 1 Objectives:

* Connect and get acquainted with the environment and members of the First Cloud AI Journey.
* Grasp the big picture of cloud computing and the AWS ecosystem.
* Successfully configure essential tools (Console, CLI) for long-term practice.

### Tasks to be carried out this week:
